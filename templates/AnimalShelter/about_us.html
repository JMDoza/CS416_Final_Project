    Mission Statement

    We hope to protect and improve the lives of animals that have been abused and abandoned and encourage people to adopt them so that these animals have an opportunity to live a proper life in a secure and loving home.

    Goals

    What we hope to accomplish:

    • Maintain quality of care for the animals within our shelter
    • Return pets to their rightful owners
    • Look for the best candidates that will home pets
    • Transparency in our adoption process
    • Make sure those adopting are educated in proper care for the pet they are adopting
